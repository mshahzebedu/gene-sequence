Tay-Sachs disease is a rare, fatal disorder most commonly diagnosed in babies around 6 months of age. Signs and Syomptoms

Common syomptoms are

  • Movement problems
  • Muscle weakness
  • Cherry-red" spots in the eyes
  • Vision and hearing loss
  • Seizures
  • Exaggerated reactions when the baby hears loud noises
  • Loss of motor skills, including turning over, crawling and sitting up
